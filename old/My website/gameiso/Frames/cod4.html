CoD 4

Leveling up is easy. All you have to do is kill and win and you get xp. If you complete a challenge, you might unlock an attachment or get some xp.

When you play sabotage, capture the flag, or search and destroy, you should use remote detonated explosives. If you are on the defending side, go to A or B. Once at A or B, place 2 C4 and watch the site from a building or something. When a person is spotted, BLOW HIM UP!!!

If you are on the attacking side, repeat AFTER you plant the bomb.
